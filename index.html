The finest in Italian Dining on the North End's famous corner! Established just after Prohibition on the corner of Hanover and Prince Streets in Boston's historic North End, the Florentine Cafe was renovated In 1995 to bring back its Old World look and charm, once again capturing the attention of the neighborhood and of passers-by alike.
