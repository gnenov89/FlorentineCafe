The finest in Italian Dining on the North End's famous corner!

Established just after Prohibition on the corner of Hanover and Prince Streets in Boston's historic North End, the Florentine Cafe was renovated In 1995 to bring back its Old World look and charm, once again capturing the attention of the neighborhood and of passers-by alike. The Florentine Cafe has offered a wonderful blend of fine Italian food and drink to visitors and residents of Hanover Street and to those walking Boston's Freedom Trail for over 70 years. It is to be the first open air, sidewalk-side cafe on Hanover Street.
